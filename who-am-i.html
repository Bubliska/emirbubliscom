Emir Bublis

Emir Bublis was born in Izmir in 1989. Turning his passion for cinema, which began at the age of 7, into a career, Emir initially wrote stories for TV projects and worked as a script doctor. His professional career began in 2015 as a jury member on the script evaluation committee for the TRT TV Films Competition.

Emir's first project was the 24-episode animated series Erdem, which he wrote and directed for TRT Çocuk between 2015 and 2017. In 2018, he wrote and directed the 30-episode short animated series Ramazan Güncesi, also for TRT Çocuk. The same year, he served as a script consultant on the animated project Dumper and Skoop. The series was broadcast on MinikaGO and on China's iQiyi video platform.

Emir's first feature film screenwriting credit was for the theatrical release Müfreze. He then wrote and directed his first English-language feature film, Spiralis. The film reached the semi-finals in a competition in Sweden and was released in the United States on Amazon Prime, Tubi TV, and Cineverse. Most recently, in 2022, he wrote and directed the 7-episode series Queen of the Seven, starring many famous Turkish actors.

Emir is currently working on his new thriller film project, Unethical Masterpiece.
